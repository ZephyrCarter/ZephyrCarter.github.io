Here is a list of all the bone and leather tools/implements that I have either made or bought, with the latest at the top

This staff I made by hand when I was 13 years old. This staff has endured heat and rain, mold and sand. I found it cleaning a neighber's yard, and I carved it down, romoving all the bark over many hours. For years now I have been seasoning it, grinding its bottom and carving it until it was completly smooth, no sand paper nessecary!
This staff, which I made a little less than a year ago as of writing this, was made from a fallen branch from Golden gate park in San Fransisco CA (yes I got permission). It is much newer and much lighter than my older white staff shown above, and I suspect it is made of spruce or pine. It is dryed out, but is covered in tool marks, which make it suceptible to cracking if bent to much. I am currently working on grinding its base on the sidewalk/road, as to make a natural boot of sorts and gradually carving/sanding its toolmarks out (yes I relent, I'll use sand paper).
